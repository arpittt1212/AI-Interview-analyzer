 Frequently Asked Questions
How does Whisper Speech-to-Text transcription operate?

We use OpenAI's Whisper model (tiny model by default to conserve memory). When you upload an audio file, PyTorch parses the waveform and decodes the audio into text using sequence-to-sequence deep learning layers.

How are the vocal pace WPM scores calculated?

Vocal speed is measured in Words Per Minute (WPM). The ideal professional speech pace is between 110 and 150 WPM. Speeds outside this range are scored slightly lower, indicating potential monotone hesitation (slow) or fast/coherence issues (fast).

What are verbal crutch words or filler words?

Verbal crutches are fillers like "um", "uh", "like", "actually", and "basically". Our NLP analyzer counts occurrences of these words in sentences. Minimizing these words shows clarity and high communication scores.

Do I need an NVIDIA GPU to run this application?

No! While NVIDIA GPU acceleration via CUDA cores improves speech processing times by up to 10x, the application incorporates a CPU fallback and High-Fidelity simulation mode, ensuring it runs on any desktop or laptop instantly.

How do I export my interview analysis report?

Navigate to any results dashboard and click "Download AI Report PDF". Our stylesheet automatically converts the dashboard cards into a printable document, triggering the browser's PDF generation utility.
